Privacy Policy

Site Visit Data

The SIP Website does not automatically collect any specific personal data about you (such as your name, phone number, or email address) that would enable us to recognise you as an individual.

For statistical purposes, this website keeps track of your visit and logs the following information: Internet protocol (IP) addresses, domain names, server addresses, names of the top-level domains from which you access the Internet (such as.gov,.com,.in, etc.), browser and operating system details, the date and time of your visit, the pages you accessed, the files you downloaded, and the previous Internet address from which you came. Unless a deliberate attempt to harm the site has been made, we do not attempt to connect these addresses to the identities of those who visit it. Except where a law enforcement agency may utilise a warrant to inspect the service provider's records, we will not identify users or their surfing habits.

If the SIP Website asks you to submit personal information, you will be made aware of the specific reasons why the information is needed and appropriate security precautions will be taken to safeguard your privacy. Any personally identifiable information provided voluntarily on the SIP Website is not sold or otherwise shared with any third party (public or private). Any information you give will be safeguarded against loss, abuse, unauthorised access or disclosure, modification, or destruction.

Cookies

When you access information on an Internet website, that website transmits a bit of software code called a cookie to your browser.
