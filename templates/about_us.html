About Us

We have designed a platform where each student will be assigned a unique Digital ID , using which we will be
able to track the student's academic performance and every registered independent organization will be able to
verify the student's status by simply using our application. The students will also be able to download their entire
academic details including their marks, degrees completed, etc in a single PDF file with a digital signature, instead
of submitting every document for manual verification . The organization can easily verify the document using the digital
signature present in the document or can verify using the document Id through an Application Programming Interface.
This system will help reduce the time usually taken for verifying documents manually and will fast-track all
scholarships and disbursement of student - government schemes.

Our Vision

To reduce the time taken and physical labour needed for verifying documents.

Our Mission

To develop a unique digital ID for the students, which will play an instrumental role in aiding the government of India
and professional institutions to verify any student's academic details with minimal effort.

Our Objectives

To provide:
• Easy verification of a document's authenticity.
• Using digital signature as a seal of genuineness.
• Effortless access to original documents.
• Helps to Fastrack scholarships and disburse Student-Government schemes.
